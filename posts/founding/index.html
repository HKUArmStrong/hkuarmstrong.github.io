Announcing the Founding of HKU ArmStrong Team

We are pleased to announce the founding of the HKU ArmStrong team, a student interest group in the HKU Innovation Wing.

April 5, 2024

  • announcement
  • HKU
  • Innovation Wing

We are pleased to announce the founding of the HKU ArmStrong team, a student interest group in the HKU Innovation Wing, founded by Ian Lo, Nafis UI, and Yoko Lau.

Our team is dedicated to exploring and advancing the field of robotics, with a focus on innovative projects and collaborative learning. We aim to provide a platform for students to engage in hands-on experiences and develop their skills in robotics and related technologies.

Stay tuned for more updates on our projects and activities!
